Material Tasks

A simple, but powerful Material Design to-do list app


Compatible with Android 4.0 Jellybean and above

Features

Simple and Elegant

Material Tasks is a to-do list app that is designed to be simple and elegant. Designed with Google's Material Design language, you will be greeted with a familiar user-interface and immediately be able to pick up and use, such as the context-based "Floating Action Button". Also, long press on list items to bring up the context delete menu. It is simply just intuitive.

Tired of mismanaging multiple lists where tasks get buried and hidden? Material Tasks keeps it simple by greeting you with one, unified list. Plus all of your tasks are stored locally on your device, so there is no need to worry about privacy or usability without network access.

Dark Mode, activated

With app theming, you can choose between "Light" and "Dark" theme and change the look and feel of the whole app. The "Dark" theme brings the color brightness down a notch, increasing readability and contrast.

Keep tabs on your tasks

You will be greeted with a clean and powerful task management screen when adding a new task. Enter the task's title, details, due date, completion status, and even take a picture to remind yourself and keep tab on the task at hand. Simply tap and type, press and capture, and long hold on the image to delete it!

Gentle, unobtrusive notification

When you set a due-date or completion date for your task, the application will remember that and prompt you with a basic and unobtrusive notification on that day. Treat it like a friendly nudge that reminds you to open the app and check the task that requires your attention.
